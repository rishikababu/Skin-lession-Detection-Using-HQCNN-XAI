AI Powered Skin Cancer Detection

An intelligent medical diagnostic system that analyzes dermatoscopic skin images using Deep Learning and provides accurate predictions, confidence scores, severity levels and Grad-CAM visual explanations.

Project Overview

Skin cancer is one of the most common and serious medical conditions worldwide. Early detection is essential for effective treatment and improved patient outcomes. This system leverages Artificial Intelligence and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to analyze skin lesion images and assist dermatologists in identifying and analyzing potential skin cancer cases quickly and accurately.

System Features

🔬 AI Image Analysis

Deep learning CNN model analyzes dermatoscopic images for disease detection.

📊 Prediction Confidence

Displays probability score indicating prediction reliability.

🔥 Grad-CAM Heatmap

Visual explanation highlighting important regions in the skin image.

📄 PDF Medical Report

Automatically generate downloadable diagnostic reports.

Skin Cancer Types Detected

Our AI model analyzes dermatoscopic skin images and detects the following seven skin lesion categories from the HAM10000 dataset.

🧬 Melanoma

Dangerous skin cancer that develops in melanocytes.

🔬 Melanocytic Nevus

Common mole that is usually benign but monitored.

🧫 Basal Cell Carcinoma

Slow growing cancer that starts in basal cells.

☀️ Actinic Keratosis

Precancerous lesion caused by sun exposure.

🩹 Benign Keratosis

Non-cancerous skin growth.

🧪 Dermatofibroma

Small benign tumor appearing as a firm bump.

🩸 Vascular Lesions

Blood vessel abnormalities such as angiomas.
